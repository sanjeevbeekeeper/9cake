How it works?

Five steps: Placing the order, Reviewing the order, Making the payment, Baking fresh, and Delivering to the address.

It starts with the customer placing their order using an online 'Order' form, once received i will review and confirming the order. Once the confirmation is done I will intimate you through WhatsApp and once the payment is made, I will starts preparing the fresh order. Finally, I will delivered to your address.

1. place your order

Use the 'Order form' to Place your order

2. order review

We will review the order as soon as I have received.

3. payment

Once your order is confirmed you can make the payment. Order will be proceed only after the full payment is made.

Payment details will be send through WhatsApp

4. fresh baking

As soon as the payment is made I make the order fresh.

5. delivery

Order will be delivered to the given address
